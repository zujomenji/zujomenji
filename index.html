_About Me

I'm a student at National University, working on my degree in BS Information Technology (BSIT-MAA). I really enjoy bringing my Digital Art skills together with technical works like User Interface (UI), User Experience (UX), and making games.

I started my professional journey as a freelance digital artist on Fiverr from 2020 to 2022. During that time, I focused on drawing fan arts, character designs, and original concepts. All the clients I worked with were happy with my art and always gave me high ratings. This work taught me a lot about making clients happy and delivering quality work. I truly love the process of being creative and getting to brainstorm new ideas.

Recently, my friend and I completed a school project: an interactive game menu we built in Unity called "Finding Aerith Fanmade," inspired by the Final Fantasy VII Remake. This project made us want to finish the whole game, and now we are planning to make more games together in the future.

I've always liked to draw and just be creative in any way I can. It's exciting how my interests have grown from traditional Arts and Graphic Design to Websites, UI/UX, and now Game Making. I want to keep learning more, creating new things, and hopefully inspire other people through my work.
